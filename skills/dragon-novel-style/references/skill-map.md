# Dragon Novel Style Skill Map

## 1. Daily-life ballast
Use clutter, errands, messages, bills, devices, food, transport, weather, family nagging, or workplace drudgery as ballast.
Purpose: make later impossibility feel earned.

## 2. Petty protagonist buffer
Let the protagonist convert pain into something smaller:
- a joke
- a shopping calculation
- a side complaint
- a mean little comparison
- a low-grade fantasy

Purpose: avoid clean melodrama.

## 3. Humiliation conversion
Convert “I got hurt” into:
- “this is ridiculous”
- “if X happened to my character...”
- “fine, whatever”
Then let the actual hurt leak later.

## 4. Concrete delivery of fate
Deliver turning points through something physical:
- letter
- email
- broken object
- package
- system notification
- account ban
- receipt
- train ticket

Purpose: keep extraordinary events tactile.

## 5. Delayed grandeur
Do not announce destiny too early.
Keep the narrative in low-ceiling reality until the last workable moment.
